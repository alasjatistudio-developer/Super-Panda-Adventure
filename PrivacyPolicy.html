Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Super Panda Adventure ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you play our mobile game.

1. Information Collection

Our game is designed to play with minimal data collection. We collect:

  • Local Game Data: Coins, levels, and unlocked items are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • Device Information: Through third-party services (Google AdMob), technical information such as device model, OS version, and unique advertising identifiers may be collected.

2. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may use advertising IDs to serve personalized ads based on your interests. For more information, please visit:

How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

3. GDPR Compliance (Data Protection Rights)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights:

  • The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.
  • The right of rectification.
  • The right to object or restrict processing.

Since we store game progress locally, you can exercise your "Right to be Forgotten" simply by clearing the App Data in your Android settings or uninstalling the app.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is intended for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. We follow the COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) guidelines provided by AdMob for child-directed apps.

5. Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
