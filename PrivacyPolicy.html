Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Mei 2026

Welcome to the royal world of Super Panda Adventure. This application is proudly operated by Alasjati Studio ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us, and we are dedicated to protecting it with the highest standards of integrity.

This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you interact with our mobile game and describes your privacy rights.

1. Information We Process

Our philosophy is built on "Privacy by Design." We collect only the absolute minimum data required to ensure a smooth, high-end gaming experience.

A. Local Game State Data

We utilize encrypted local device storage (SharedPreferences) to manage your progression. This data remains on your physical device at all times and includes:

  • Your Gold Coin balance and overall Royal Treasury achievements.
  • Character collection status (Ninja, Royal, King, and Standard Pandas).
  • In-game power-up inventory (Magnets, Royal Shields).
  • World exploration progress and level-based unlocks.
  • Competitive high scores and Player Rank titles.

Note: Super Panda Adventure does not maintain centralized servers for player data. Clearing your application data or uninstalling the game will result in the permanent loss of this progress.

2. Third-Party Integration

To support our free-to-play model and analyze game stability, we partner with industry-leading service providers who may collect non-identifiable device information.

  • Google AdMob: Facilitates the delivery of premium advertisements. They may use unique device identifiers to provide relevant content and prevent fraudulent activity.
    Review Google's Privacy Standards
  • Firebase Analytics: Enables us to analyze technical performance, user retention, and gameplay balancing. This allows us to maintain a stable, high-performance experience.
    Review Firebase Data Practices

3. Global Compliance (GDPR)

For our esteemed players in the European Economic Area (EEA), we strictly adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have full control over your data.

Because we do not store personal identifiers on our systems, you can exercise your "Right to Erasure" instantly by resetting the application data in your Android system settings or uninstalling the app.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Super Panda Adventure is a safe environment for players of all ages. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take immediate steps to remove it.

5. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request information about the collection and sale of your personal information. We do not "sell" personal information as defined by the CCPA.

6. Data Security Standards

We implement robust, industry-standard security protocols to safeguard your local data. While no digital interaction is completely immune to risks, we strive to ensure your royal treasury and progress are as secure as possible.

7. Policy Updates

We may periodically update this policy to reflect changes in our game or legal requirements. Any revisions will be signified by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
